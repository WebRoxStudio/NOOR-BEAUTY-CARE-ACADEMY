Our Story

Where Beauty
Becomes Art

Welcome to Noor Beauty Care & Academy — Dahod's most trusted name in beauty services and professional beauty education. Founded with a passion for enhancing every woman's natural radiance, we have served hundreds of clients and trained the next generation of beauty professionals.

Our salon is built on the belief that every woman deserves to feel confident and beautiful. Our expert team uses premium international products and modern techniques to deliver flawless results — from everyday grooming to show-stopping bridal transformations.

Beyond services, our Academy is a launchpad for aspiring beauty professionals. With hands-on training, certified courses, and mentorship from seasoned experts, we've helped over 200 students build successful careers in the beauty industry.
